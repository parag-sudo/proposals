The Problem

Why Sales Training Fails at the Point of Application

Salespeople learn theory, but have no tool to guide behaviour in live client interactions. The result is consistent and costly.

🔄

Habit Reversion Under Pressure

Without real-time guidance, salespeople abandon training the moment a live call gets uncomfortable and default to old patterns.

🎯

Bias-Driven Assessment

Instead of structured discovery, salespeople make gut-feel judgements — leading to missed opportunities and wrong qualification signals.

💰

Premature Price Negotiation

Conversations drift to "what's your discount?" before value is established, destroying deal quality and margin.

📋

Absent or Inaccurate Reporting

Salespeople hate daily reports. Managers get fabricated summaries. Meeting intelligence is lost — especially when reps leave.

💸

CRM Tools Are Too Expensive

Salesforce costs ₹3,000+ per user/month after crores in customisation. Zoho is cheaper but still overkill for a 3–6 person sales team.

👤

Prospect Data Walks Out the Door

When a salesperson resigns, their entire prospect history disappears. No records, no context, no continuity.

The Solution

What Sales Meta Does

A procedural, selection-based coaching layer that runs live during a meeting — guiding without recording, structured without AI overhead.

🌳

Question Tree Engine

Hierarchical question flows built on SPIN methodology. Each response branches to the next right question — keeping the salesperson on track.

📱

Live Meeting Interface

Tap-to-select responses during the meeting. No typing in front of the client. Visual progress through 4 structured stages.

📡

Full Offline Mode

Pre-download question trees before heading into the field. Works in factory floors, shadow areas, remote locations. Auto-syncs when back online.

📊

Auto-Generated Reports

Click "End Meeting" — a structured report is instantly generated and sent to the manager. No manual input, no fabricated summaries.

🏢

Multi-Tenant SaaS

One platform, many clients. Each tenant gets full data isolation, their own question trees, users, and configuration panel.

🧭

Contextual Mentoring

Based on which response is selected, the app can surface inline coaching tips — surfacing Rakesh's methodology at exactly the right moment.

Scope of Work

What We're Building

Full-stack delivery across mobile, backend, admin panel, and infrastructure.

🏗️

Tenant Management

  • Tenant onboarding and registration (Super Admin)
  • Tenant-level settings and configuration
  • Full data isolation per tenant
  • Subscription and plan management per tenant
  • Super Admin platform-wide dashboard
🔐

Auth & User Management

  • Role-based login: Super Admin, Tenant Admin, Salesperson
  • Salesperson creation and management by Tenant Admin
  • JWT-based auth with refresh token support
  • Password reset flow
🌳

Question Tree Engine

  • Tree-based hierarchical question and response builder
  • Branching logic per response (leads to next question)
  • Multiple question trees per tenant
  • Draft and publish states per tree
  • Support for 4-level SPIN structure (S/P/I/N)
🎬

Scenario Management

  • Create and manage named sales scenarios
  • Assign question trees to scenarios
  • Assign scenarios to salespeople or teams
  • Example: Cold Call, Follow-up, Renewal
📱

Live Meeting Interface (Mobile)

  • Start meeting with client/prospect details
  • Select scenario before meeting begins
  • Tree-based Q&A navigation during meeting
  • Tap to select current question and client response
  • Visual progress indicator showing meeting stage
  • Contextual guidance (mentoring mode)
  • No audio recording — selection-based input only
📡

Offline Mode

  • Pre-download full session question tree before meeting
  • Full functionality without internet during active meeting
  • Auto-sync session data when connectivity restored
  • Conflict resolution on sync
📊

Post-Meeting Reporting

  • Auto-generate report on session close
  • Captures questions asked, responses selected, path taken
  • Meeting score generated based on response patterns
  • Accessible to Tenant Admin across all salespeople
  • Prospect data retained independently of salesperson status
⚙️

Admin Configuration Panel (Web)

  • Question builder with branching logic
  • Manage question trees and scenarios
  • Assign scenarios to users and teams
  • View and filter meeting reports
  • Manage prospects and client records
  • Manage users within the tenant
👤

Prospects Management

  • Create and manage prospect records
  • View full meeting history per prospect
  • Data persists even when associated salesperson is deactivated
📚

Content & Resources

  • Upload and manage reference material per tenant (PDFs, text)
  • Contextual tips surfaced during meeting based on selected path
  • Author's book content accessible in-app chapter-wise
🔔

Notifications

  • Push notifications to salespeople (session reminders)
  • In-app and email to Tenant Admin (new reports available)
🎛️

Super Admin Panel

  • Create, view, suspend, and restore tenants
  • Platform-wide usage and activity dashboard

⛔ Out of Scope (this phase)

  • AI or machine learning features in the live application
  • Real-time AI analysis during meetings
  • Audio or video recording
  • CRM integrations (Salesforce, Zoho, etc.)
  • Native desktop application
  • Custom white-labelling per tenant
  • Payment gateway integration (future phase)
